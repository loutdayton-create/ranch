The Cottonwood Ranch Experience

Welcome to Cottonwood Ranch, a working cattle ranch nestled in the heart of Wyoming's wilderness. For over three decades, our family has been sharing the authentic spirit of the American West with guests from around the world.

Experience genuine ranch life on our 500+ acre property, where you'll participate in daily cattle operations, explore pristine mountain trails on horseback, and cast your line in crystal-clear streams teeming with native trout.

At 6,200 feet elevation, our ranch offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Teton Mountain Range, star-filled skies unmarred by city lights, and the kind of peaceful solitude that allows you to truly disconnect and reconnect with what matters most.
